Airports

There are two airports within 1 hour of downtown Toronto. They are:

  • Toronto Pearson (YYZ): 30 minutes from downtown.
  • Toronto Island / Billy Bishop (YTZ): 10 minutes from downtown.

Unless you are travelling from the East Coast of North America, it is most likely that you will find flights into Pearson instead of Billy Bishop.
